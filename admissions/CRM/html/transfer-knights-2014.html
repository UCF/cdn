Transfer to be a Knight

Transferring to UCF can be a smooth process for students but it starts with preparation. That's why we're offering "Transfer to be a Knight", an information session specifically for transfer students.

To learn about the application process, transcript and credit review, advising and support services, and mentorships, join representatives from the Office of Undergraduate Admissions from 4-6 p.m. on select Thursdays.

For more information, and to register, visit admissions.ucf.edu/transferknights
