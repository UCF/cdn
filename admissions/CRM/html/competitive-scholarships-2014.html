Competitive Scholarships

Kiplinger's and The Princeton Review ranked a UCF education as one of the nation's best values in part because 48 percent of students graduate without any educational debt. And UCF is ranked among the top 30 colleges in the nation and number one in Florida for enrolled National Merit Scholars.

Other highlights:
  • A variety of scholarships – from $12,000 to $36,000 per year – are given to top academic achievers
  • More than $400 million in scholarships, grants and loans is awarded annually
  • Need-based financial aid and scholarships are awarded to seven out of every 10 students
  • Partnerships with industry leaders connect you to work-study, internship and part- and full-time job opportunities
To learn more about UCF scholarships and financial assistance, visit the Office of Student Financial Assistance website.
